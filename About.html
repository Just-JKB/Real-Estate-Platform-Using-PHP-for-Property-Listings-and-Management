Clavem

is a wide range of property choices located in Lipa City area, for all types of buyers and investor. Our selection of foreclosed properties includes House and Lots, Apartments, Townhouses, Condominium Units, Office Spaces, Buildings, Vacant Lots, Warehouses, as well as Agricultural Lands, Commercial Spaces, and Industrial Sites. We sort our listings through a color coding system Green, Yellow and, Red.

GREEN TAG:

Real estate properties that assure buyers a value-for money purchase and an efficient and worry-free post-sale documentation process.

  • Titles / Tax Declarations registered under the name of the Bank.
  • In possession
  • With For Sale signages
  • With authorized caretakers

YELLOW TAG:

We offer real estate properties with "Special Concerns" which we readily discloses to interested buyers. Examples may include, but are not limited to;

  • Titles / Tax Declarations in process of consolidation
  • Titles with technical description errors, Reconstitution (Sec. 7 RA 26), Sec. 4 Rule 74
  • No possession of property (with former owner, tenants, or illegal settlers)

RED TAG:

We value your needs and concerns, thus our commitment to transparency and full disclosure of all material information.

  • Pending court cases against the Bank filed by the previous owner or 3rd party claimants.
  • Titles with annotation/s, as to adverse claims or Lis pendens
